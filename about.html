How It All Began

Founded in 2018, LuxStore started as a passion project between three friends who were frustrated with fast fashion's disposable mentality. We wanted to create clothing that would last — both in quality and in style.

Today, we're proud to offer a curated collection of minimalist essentials that form the backbone of a thoughtful wardrobe. Each piece is designed to work across seasons and occasions.
